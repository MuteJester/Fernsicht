Fernsicht

Peer-to-peer progress tracking

Open a link with #room=<id> to view progress,

or #room=<id>&role=broadcaster to send.

from fernsicht import blick

for item in blick(data, desc="Working"):
    process(item)

A shareable link will appear in your terminal.
